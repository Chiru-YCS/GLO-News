GLO-NEWS is dedicated to bringing you the latest news articles from around the world. Our mission is to provide accurate and timely information to our readers.

This website provides the latest news articles from around the world, powered by the News API.Users can search for articles based on specific queries, allowing them to stay updated on topics of interest.

The application is built using Flask, a lightweight web framework for Python, and utilizes Bootstrap for responsive design.

Feel free to explore the latest news and stay informed!
